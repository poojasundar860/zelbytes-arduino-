## Port Issues

* Wrong COM port selected.
* Faulty or power-only USB cable.
* Loose USB connection.
* Missing drivers.

## Baud Rate Issues

* Baud rate mismatch with `Serial.begin()`.
* Garbled or no Serial Monitor output.
* Common rates: 9600, 115200.

## Brownout Symptoms

* Unexpected resets.
* Repeated startup messages.
* LED flickering.
* Random restarts or unstable operation.
