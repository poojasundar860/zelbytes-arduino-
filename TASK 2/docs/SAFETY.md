Manual Shutoff Location

A manual ball valve is installed upstream of the solenoid valve and serves as the primary manual shutoff. It must remain accessible at all times and be closed before maintenance, wiring changes, or emergency situations.

Safety Rules

The solenoid valve is controlled only through the relay module.
The valve remains de-energized during startup.
An emergency stop (ESTOP) command immediately turns the valve OFF.
A drip tray is placed under the bench during testing.
Relay operation is verified before connecting the valve.
