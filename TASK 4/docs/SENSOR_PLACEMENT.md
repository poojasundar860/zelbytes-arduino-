# Sensor Placement Notes

## LDR

- Mounted facing ambient light.
- Avoided shadows from nearby objects.
- Placement documented with setup photograph.

---

## DHT22

- Positioned away from direct sunlight.
- Kept away from relay and solenoid valve heat sources.
- Protected from direct water spray and mist.

---

## HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor

- Mounted facing the target surface.
- Clear line of sight maintained.
- No obstacles within the measurement path.

---

## Soil Moisture Sensor

- Probe inserted approximately 5 cm into the soil.
- Avoided contact with pot walls and stones.
- Calibration performed using dry and saturated soil samples.

---

## General Notes

- Sensor wires kept short and secure.
- All sensors powered from the Arduino 5V supply.
- Wiring verified before data logging.
